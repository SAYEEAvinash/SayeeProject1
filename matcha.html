Discover Matcha Latte

The Matcha Latte is a calming, vibrant green tea latte made from finely ground matcha powder, steamed milk, and a touch of sweetness. It's a healthy and flavorful alternative to regular coffee lattes.

Ingredients

  • High-Quality Matcha Powder
  • Steamed Milk
  • Filtered Water
  • Sweetener (optional)

Vegan Additions

  • Oat Milk
  • Almond Milk
  • Coconut Milk

Gluten-Free Options

  • Certified Gluten-Free Matcha Powder
  • Organic Syrups (Vanilla, Hazelnut, Maple)
