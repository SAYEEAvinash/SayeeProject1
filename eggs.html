Discover the Club Sandwich

Our Club Sandwich is a wholesome, satisfying meal, made with freshly toasted bread, creamy avocado, and perfectly cooked eggs. It's a perfect balance of flavors, offering a healthy and delicious twist on the traditional club sandwich. Ideal for any time of the day!

  • Perfectly Cooked Eggs (Fried or Scrambled)
  • Ripe Avocado
