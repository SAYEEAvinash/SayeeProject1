Discover the Club Sandwich

The Club Sandwich is a classic American triple-decker sandwich made with layers of crispy bacon, tender turkey, fresh lettuce, juicy tomato, and creamy mayo, all piled between three slices of toasted bread. It's the perfect balance of flavors and textures in every bite, making it a must-try for any sandwich lover.

Ingredients

  • Toasted White Bread
  • Crispy Bacon
  • Grilled Turkey Breast
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise

Vegan Additions

  • Vegan Bacon
  • Vegan Mayo
  • Toasted Multigrain or Gluten-Free Bread

Gluten-Free Options

  • Gluten-Free Bread
  • Gluten-Free Turkey
