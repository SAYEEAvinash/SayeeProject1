Discover Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is a classic comfort food made with toasted bread and melted cheese. The perfect balance of crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside, this dish is a favorite for all ages. It pairs well with tomato soup or a fresh salad for the ultimate meal.

Ingredients

  • Freshly Sliced Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • American Cheese

Vegan Additions

  • Vegan Butter
  • Vegan Cheese (e.g., Daiya, Follow Your Heart)

Gluten-Free Options

  • Gluten-Free Bread
  • Organic Gluten-Free Cheese
