Welcome to Brew & Bliss Cafe

            At Brew & Bliss Cafe, we believe in serving more than just coffee; we offer an experience. 
            Nestled in the heart of our community, our cafe is built on the foundation of sustainability, quality, and passion for coffee.
            We are proud to source our coffee beans directly from the lush plantations of Coorg, India’s coffee capital. 
            Our beans are grown under the shade of towering rainforest trees, nurtured with care, 
            and hand-picked by local farmers who have mastered the art of coffee cultivation over generations.
            Staying true to our values, all our offerings are 100% organic. 
            This not only ensures an unparalleled coffee experience but also supports the health of our planet and the well-being of our farmers.
            From farm to cup, we strive to maintain transparency and integrity in everything we do. 
            Whether you’re here for a quick espresso shot or a leisurely latte, know that every sip supports a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
            Come, join us in celebrating the rich flavors of Coorg and the beauty of organic living at Brew & Bliss Cafe!
